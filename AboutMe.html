Introduction

I’m Ben, and I’ve lived in Ohio my whole life. After high school I attended college to study Media Arts and Animation, however after a time I decided to take a hiatus and reevaluate my career plans. As I reflected on my past, I decided that I wanted to change course and go down another route.


I have had a passion for technology from a young age. I enjoyed doing things such as building my own computer, writing simple scripts, and troubleshooting my own technical problems. From childhood I wanted to work in the game industry and was originally going to school to become a 3D modeler. This all lead me to the conclusion that I still wanted to work in the game industry but instead wanted to code. Based on this I decided that I would go into Information Technology with a focus in Game Design and Simulation. Coupled with my knowledge in media arts and animation I feel I will be an asset to any company in the gaming industry.
